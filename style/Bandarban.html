Bandarban

Bandarban is one of the most beautiful and naturally rich places in Bangladesh. It lies in the Chittagong Hill Tracts and is often called the “Roof of Bangladesh” because of its high hills and scenic landscapes.


  1. Lush Green Hills – The rolling green hills stretch as far as the eye can see. Covered in forests, the hills change color with the seasons, making every visit unique.

  2. Mystic Clouds and Cool Air – Places like Nilgiri and Nilachal often sit above the clouds. Standing there feels like you’re floating in the sky.

  3. Waterfalls and Lakes – Bandarban is home to beautiful waterfalls like Nafakhum and Tinap Saitar, and tranquil spots like Boga Lake surrounded by hills.

  4. Unique Culture – The area is rich in tribal culture, including the Marma and Bawm communities. Their colorful clothing, wooden homes, and peaceful lifestyle add cultural charm.

  5. Golden Temple (Buddha Dhatu Jadi) – A beautiful golden Buddhist temple on a hill that glows under sunlight and offers a peaceful view of the surrounding nature.
